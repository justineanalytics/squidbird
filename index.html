WHAT IS SQUIDBIRD?

THE PLATFORM

  • Aggregates post-level data from any X, TikTok, or Instagram account (except those listed as private)
  • Automatically calculates engagement and impressions totals and averages for time period
  • Exports all available metadata (including hard-to-find metrics like Instagram stories metrics and non-owned post impressions) in CSV format for further analysis

THE SERVICE—Justine as agency of one

  • 13 years of experience in social media—though analytics is my specialty, I have deep experience in social strategy, content curation, creative technology solutions, & social research
  • Led social media strategy for dozens of Fortune 500 brands, including: lululemon, Gatorade, Red Bull, Google Security, Taco Bell, Target, Venmo, & Microsoft
  • Acts as a full-service social media strategy team, covering social playbooks, reporting, ad optimization, A/B testing, & my secret weapon—custom automation of repeated processes like research & reporting via AI

WHY SQUIDBIRD?

Squidbird saves money while enhancing statistical rigor and data transparency.

  • Squidbird passes the costs saved by using AI for preliminary analysis off to the buyer, unlike agencies, which keep the profit for themselves.
  • The platform (automated reports) is ideal for customers that already have a social agency in place, but want to measure the agency's creative performance with low-cost, unbiased reporting. Never let an agency report on their own creative content—when agencies measure themselves, there is usually internal pressure to present findings in a way that benefits the agency, even when KPIs are in decline.
  • The service is ideal for customers that need both reporting + an expert Social Strategist to guide decision-making or create a playbook.
